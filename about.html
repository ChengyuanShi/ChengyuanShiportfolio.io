My name is Chengyuan Shi. I am an international student from China. I have been interested in anime since I was a child. So I chose a related major in design at university. These works of art were produced by me in the past year. My artworks contain Glitch art, Composite art, Web art, and code art. They were produced by Audacity, Photoshop, Atom, and Premiere. These works are collectively referred to as digital media art, and I use these tools to make and share my ideas and my daily life.
