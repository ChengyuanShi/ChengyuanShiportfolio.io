Composite Art, Digital Image, 2020

In this series, I decided to present the theme of the work through the use of dynamic posters. I portrayed the virus as visible so that it is more intuitive to feel that the virus is actually hiding around us. I also will depict people's inner struggles of the epidemic in an abstract way. In the posters, I presented the characters like shadows, which do not represent a specific person but can be anyone. The poster has the words "stay home" on it. I used this to remind people to take the epidemic seriously. After all, staying at home is the safest and most effective strategy to fight the epidemic.
